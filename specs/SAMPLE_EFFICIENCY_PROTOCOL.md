# Sample-Efficiency Telemetry and Prior-Authority Sidecar

Status: normative non-blocking telemetry and authority sidecar for V0-040 and later
automatic-coordinate or partial/learned-dynamics construction.

Implemented V0-040 prior profiles:

```text
source_unanimous_exact_v1
nonproduction_external_control_v1
sample_evidence_taxonomy_v1
```

The project objective remains to synthesize one reusable auditable abstract world
model, plan mainly in that model, and recover ground distinctions only at a failed
value/risk proof frontier. The main construction line may continue through automatic
coordinate synthesis and partial or learned dynamics without first solving the
cold-start sample tax.

This sidecar has a narrower purpose. It prevents later work from losing the evidence
needed to diagnose that tax, and prevents a prior from becoming an unacknowledged
certificate authority. It does not prescribe a sample-saving operator before the
actual construction algorithm and its bottlenecks are observable.

## 1. Non-blocking status

The cold-start cost of discovering a reusable abstraction is a registered research
risk, not a prerequisite Gate for the current construction line. Compression,
exactness, reuse, or a successful held-out proposal does not establish sample
efficiency; conversely, the absence of a sample-efficiency result does not block work
on:

- automatic coordinate and predicate proposal;
- exact quotient synthesis and independent certification;
- partial, value-aware, or learned dynamics;
- certificate-directed local recovery; or
- end-to-end world-model planning and fallback.

Once those paths expose their real access pattern, a later ledger revision may define
an operator that reduces the dominant evidence cost. Such an operator must be judged
against the unoptimized path using the frozen telemetry below; it must not be assumed
in advance.

The current Gate state is deliberately non-blocking:

```text
SAMPLE_EFFICIENCY_GATE_NOT_RUN
sample_efficiency_gate_blocks_mainline = false
```

## 2. Frozen evidence-event taxonomy

Every transition-like event belongs to exactly one of the following classes. The
class names are normative and may not be aliased to the generic word `sample` in an
official artifact.

### ENVIRONMENT_INTERACTION

A newly executed transition in the target environment whose outcome was not already
available to the algorithm. This includes a real-world interaction or the registered
environment-facing analogue in an experiment. Re-reading or replaying its archived
record is not another environment interaction.

### GENERATIVE_ORACLE_SAMPLE

One stochastic outcome requested from a generative oracle at a chosen state-action
input. It is an information-bearing oracle sample but is not an on-trajectory target
environment interaction. Repeated requests count separately.

### EXACT_KERNEL_QUERY

One invocation of an authoritative exact transition interface, including deterministic
table lookup or exact enumeration of an outcome distribution. Repeated queries count
separately. An exact kernel query is model-access work, not an interaction sample and
not a generative-oracle sample.

### OFFLINE_LOGGED_OBSERVATION

One immutable transition observation already present in a source or target offline
dataset before the relevant online run. Repeated reads do not create new observations;
their I/O remains chargeable under the workload-accounting contract. Source and
target logs must remain distinguishable by lane and manifest.

### SYNTHETIC_MODEL_ROLLOUT

One transition generated by a learned, fitted, approximate, or abstract model rather
than by the target environment or authoritative oracle. It is computational evidence
for proposal or planning only. It cannot increase empirical target coverage by being
relabelled as data.

Every future official sample-efficiency artifact must report all five classes as
separate non-negative integer counters, including native zeros. Missing is not zero.
V0-040 is a narrower pre-Gate exact-authority control: its typed accounting materializes
`interaction_samples`, `exact_ground_kernel_calls`, distinct and eligible state-action
rows, and candidate evaluations for the source and target lanes. It does not claim a
counter-complete five-class WorkVector. The absent classes cannot be inferred as native
zero records or used to open the sample-efficiency Gate. Later implementations may add
other compute/I/O diagnostics, but those diagnostics may not replace the five evidence
classes in an official matched-budget experiment.

No weighted sum of the classes is official in V0-040.

## 3. Evidence lanes

Every evidence event also belongs to exactly one lane:

```text
offline_source
online_target
operational_query
standalone_evaluation
```

`offline_source` contains source-task logs, source interaction or oracle collection,
meta-prior fitting, feature generation, and source-only validation. This work is not
free; it is retained for later workload amortization, but it does not enter target
online counters.

`online_target` contains target adaptation and target-directed evidence acquisition.
`operational_query` contains evidence access required to plan, certify, locally recover,
or fall back for a registered query. `standalone_evaluation` contains held-out audit
and comparison work and may not refit or improve a frozen production artifact.

An event may not migrate between lanes after its outcome is observed. A target held-out
label, query result, certificate, value, policy, or J0 result may not be imported into
`offline_source` and then presented as a source-frozen prior.

## 4. Proposal-only prior and broad-support metadata

A human, learned, language-model, meta-learned, or so-called philosophical prior is a
proposal mechanism only. It may rank or generate typed candidate coordinates,
predicates, partitions, semantic actions, model families, or acquisition locations.
It may not:

- narrow a sound reward, risk, or transition envelope;
- certify a quotient, partial model, plan, feasibility, or infeasibility;
- suppress a target counterexample or remove ground fallback;
- use target QuerySpec, Q/value, policy, J0, or held-out labels as a hidden construction
  channel; or
- turn posterior mass, disagreement, or a language-model judgment into proof evidence.

V0-040 production freezes a source-unanimous preferred hypothesis and records positive
mass for both that hypothesis and every member of the complete 4096-member V0-039
catalogue. The `wide_tail_base_mass` and derived hypothesis masses are support
metadata only. V0-040 does **not** execute a proposal schedule, enumerate the tail on
the held-out target, or claim that the metadata prevents search starvation. The target
audits exactly one source-unanimous proposal.

Any later executable wide-tail scheduler must separately freeze its merge order,
tie-breaks, deduplication, finite cap and target-access accounting before it can claim
coverage or sample savings. Until then, broad support cannot be cited as executed
exploration, a fallback guarantee, or a sample-efficiency mechanism.

A stale, malformed, unsupported, or mismatched prior produces a typed prior-mismatch
result. It publishes no quotient/model certificate and returns control to the normal
construction, local-recovery, or ground-fallback path. Prior mismatch is not evidence
of infeasibility.

## 5. V0-040 exact first-certificate control

V0-040 is an authority and leakage control, not a sample-efficiency experiment. A
source-frozen prior proposes an existing registered feature subset for a distinct
held-out LMB support task. The target exact-kernel audit is the sole acceptance
authority and, on success, constructs the quotient and portable RAPM for reuse.

Required properties are:

1. source and target task and coverage IDs are distinct and content-addressed; the
   exact control may intentionally share one structural kernel family;
2. every production source exact audit accepts the identical candidate, and the
   source-unanimous prior freezes before target access;
3. source ground rows are not copied into the target audit;
4. no target query, value, policy, J0, certificate, or behavioural signature enters
   the proposal channel;
5. independent exact target reconstruction is the only acceptance authority;
6. accepted target accounting separates interaction samples, exact-query repeats,
   distinct/eligible state-action inputs and proposal evaluations; and
7. a second in-coverage query reuses the unchanged certified model.

The certificate scope explicitly records:

```text
global_minimality = false
```

Exact acceptance proves the registered homomorphism obligations for the covered target
support. It does not prove that the proposed coordinates are globally minimal, that
the system invented the grammar, that interaction samples were reduced, or that the
method generalizes across domains.

Required negative controls include source/target task or coverage leakage, a
non-unanimous or caller-substituted production proposal, prior mismatch, candidate and
identity tampering, duck-typed artifacts, and a coherently re-signed result whose target
audit/model cannot be independently rebuilt. The non-production external-proposal
profile may exercise a deliberately wrong candidate, but it cannot mint production
evidence.

## 6. Mainline learned/partial dynamics authority

The mainline may now implement sampled, partial, value-aware, value-equivalent, or
learned dynamics without a prior sample-efficiency Gate. The resulting model remains
certifiable only relative to its preregistered scope, including as applicable:

- reward, cost, failure, and terminal bases;
- semantic actions and concretizer;
- policy, value, model, or proof-function family;
- support, horizon, and distributional coverage; and
- confidence allocation and fail-closed fallback rule.

Changing that scope changes the model identity and invalidates claims outside the new
certificate. A learned model, posterior, ensemble, or synthetic rollout may propose
where the proof should inspect next, but cannot certify its own soundness.

Telemetry instrumentation should be attached as these algorithms are built. It must
not restructure the scientific algorithm merely to optimize an as-yet hypothetical
sample Gate.

## 7. Deferred sample-efficiency experiment

The official matched-budget protocol is intentionally deferred until the construction
and recovery paths are executable and their dominant evidence costs can be measured.
At that point a new normative ledger revision must freeze, before results are visible:

- the direct ground optimization baseline and its information interface;
- the unoptimized abstraction/world-model baseline;
- any proposed heuristic, meta-prior, or certificate-directed operator;
- source/target/OOD splits and offline-cost treatment;
- matched target evidence budgets and prefix checkpoints;
- plan/certificate/noncertificate coverage and fallback denominators; and
- the exact vector-valued comparison or separately ratified scalar rule.

The comparison must give methods the same registered information authority. In
particular, environment interactions, generative-oracle samples, exact-kernel queries,
offline logged observations, and synthetic rollouts may not be substituted for one
another by matching only a generic count. A direct optimizer and an abstraction method
must be compared on the interfaces each is actually permitted to call, with every
evidence class visible.

The later experiment should include a direct baseline, the observed unoptimized
mainline, and each proposed tax-reduction operator. No `N_break_even` or sample-saving
claim is emitted until that protocol is ratified and run.

## 8. Gate state

V0-040 closes only the source-unanimous-prior proposal/certificate-authority boundary
and installs the evidence telemetry needed for later diagnosis. It does not execute a
wide-tail search, close, or block the future sample-efficiency Gate.

```text
official_execution_allowed = false
official_scalar_cost = null
official_N_break_even = null
WORKLOAD_ECONOMICS_GATE_NOT_RUN
COUNTER_COMPLETENESS_GATE_NOT_RUN
SAMPLE_EFFICIENCY_GATE_NOT_RUN

```

The first six locked fields retain their existing project-wide meanings. The final
boolean is specific to this sidecar: automatic-coordinate synthesis, partial/learned
dynamics, certification, and end-to-end construction may proceed while the sample
Gate remains `NOT_RUN`.
